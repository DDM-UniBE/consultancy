What to expect

These sessions are designed as a lightweight entry point for companies seeking quick, high-level insights without committing to a full consultancy project.

  • One-on-one discussions with clinicians, data scientists and digital medicine experts.
  • Rapid advisory sessions for strategic or technical questions.
  • Feedback on product concepts, algorithms or clinical use cases.
  • Independent expert opinion from an academic and hospital perspective.
  • Early-stage guidance for start-ups and innovation teams.
